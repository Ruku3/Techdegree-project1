Wheels, walks and now coding

The fresh crisp air of the bush is where I love to breathe. Mountain bike riding or bush walking were the two things I enjoyed the most with my spare time.

Then along came a little programming language called Python. A few interesting online tutorials later, including CodeAcademy and Coursera, and I was hooked.

Now my favourite thing to do in my spare time is learn to code. I still find time to bush walk and ride my bike but my list has grown from Wheels and Walks to Wheels, walks and code.

I have joined the Treehouse family and am progressing through the tech degree with the aim to make a career out of it but mostly to keep on learning, never stop.
